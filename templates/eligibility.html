Eligibility Guidelines

NMU Housing is available to undergraduate, faculty, and employees of NMU University.

Undergraduates

To qualify for housing, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • Studying in the University
  • Lives 90 km or more away from the university

Not Eligible

These groups are not eligible to lease our apartments, but they may apply to sublet from a current resident:

  • Graduates
  • NMU Summer School students
  • NMU Military education or other events

Faculty and Employees

To qualify for housing, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • Full-time, working a minimum of 35 hours per week with an active New Mansoura University ID number, receiving a New Mansoura University paycheck, and eligible for full University benefits
  • Full-time NMU appointee with an active New Mansoura University ID, which you have if you are a:
    • Visiting faculty member, scholar, or fellow
    • Benefits-eligible postdoctoral fellow

If you are currently affiliated with NMU and are starting a new job, you must submit your new appointment letter to us as part of your rental application so we can confirm eligibility. Affiliation must be valid through March 1, 2025.

Authorized Occupants

Occupants may live with a spouse/domestic partner, child, parent, or sibling, all of which are considered “Authorized Occupants.”

When applying, there will be two options to add someone to your application. Authorized Occupants should be indicated with the 'Non-Affiliated Occupant' option.

If you add an Authorized Occupant after you apply and sign a lease, you must submit an Authorized Occupant Form to the Leasing Office. Please contact the Leasing Office at the university for more information or to request an Authorized Occupant Form.
